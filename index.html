How We Operate

Preservation Framework: From Farm to Foreign Customs

Our operational model bridges smallholder clusters to sophisticated global border checks through technical compliance and modern post-harvest engineering handling styles.

Sourcing & Farmer Alignment

We deliberately partner with verified local farmer clusters and progressive Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) spanning high-yield regions across Gujarat and Maharashtra.

MRL Traceability Mandate

To guarantee frictionless customs arrivals, we supply farmers with strict, customized pre-harvest directives specifying permissible pesticide classes. This precise intervention keeps agro-chemical parameters securely within European Union and GCC Maximum Residue Limits (MRLs).

Maritime Ocean Shipments

We execute multi-port container clearances seamlessly from prominent Gujarat maritime nodes. Ocean freight routes utilize trusted customs clearing agents shipping container lots protected under a highly structured, minimum guaranteed price matrix to insulate from spot market volatility.

Priority Air Consignments

For highly time-sensitive payloads, we maintain an established operational relationship with premier, IATA-approved freight forwarders stationed at Ahmedabad and Mumbai airports. This infrastructure provides our buyers with fixed, guaranteed space allotments on commercial air carriers scheduled every Tuesday and Thursday.

Flexible Commercial Payment Plans

Structured financial workflows designed to support transparent client onboarding and risk mitigation during business scaling.

Phase 1: Initial Relationship Onboarding

Applicable for the first 3 to 5 sample air-cargo batches: Conducted strictly under 100% Advance Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) terms. This ensures immediate operational funding for meticulous farm sourcing and packhouse scheduling.

Phase 2: Established Operational Rhythm

Once standard shipping regularities are verified, we transition our trade partners onto extended transaction lines. Options extend to Document Against Payment (DP) at Sight or an Irrevocable Letter of Credit (LC), confirmed reliably through Tier-1 international banks.
