How We Operate

FPO Partnerships

We partner with local Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) across Gujarat and Maharashtra. By working directly at the source, we ensure our farmers follow precise guidelines on pesticide usage to comply with international Maximum Residue Limits (MRLs).

Preservation Framework

Our cold-chain management guarantees 100% shelf-life integrity from farm to foreign customs. We implement technical packhouse cooling immediately post-harvest to ensure premium quality upon arrival.

Logistics & Supply Chain

End-to-End Flow

  • Farm / Mandi Sourcing & Quality Inspection
  • APEDA Approved Packhouse Processing
  • Third-party refrigerated (Reefer) transportation
  • Final delivery to Indian Port / Cargo terminal
  • Fixed space allotment on premium Air/Sea freight
