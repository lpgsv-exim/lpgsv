How We Operate

FPO Partnerships

We partner with local Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) across Gujarat and Maharashtra. By working directly at the source, we ensure our farmers follow precise guidelines on pesticide usage to comply with European and GCC Maximum Residue Limits (MRLs).

Preservation Framework

Our cold-chain management guarantees 100% shelf-life integrity from farm to foreign customs. We implement technical packhouse cooling immediately post-harvest to ensure premium quality upon arrival.

Logistics & Supply Chain

Seamless Flow

  1. Farm / Mandi Sourcing
  2. Lease Packhouse Slot (APEDA approved)
  3. Hire Third Party Reefer Truck
  4. Deliver to Indian Air/Sea Port

Air Freight Scheduling

We have secured reliable relationships with IATA-approved freight forwarders at Ahmedabad and Mumbai airports. This provides our buyers with fixed space allotments on commercial flights every Tuesday and Thursday.

Payment Terms & Policies

  • • Initial Phase: 100% Advance Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) for the first 3 to 5 sample air-cargo batches.
  • • Established Phase: Once a rhythm is established, we operate under Document Against Payment (DP) at Sight or an Irrevocable Letter of Credit (LC) confirmed by an international bank.
