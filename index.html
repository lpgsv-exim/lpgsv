Our Workflow

Air Consignments

We partner with IATA-approved freight forwarders at Ahmedabad/Mumbai airports, providing fixed space allotments on commercial flights every Tuesday and Thursday.

Sea Connectivity

Strategic connectivity through Mundra and JNPT ports. We ship containers under strict minimum guaranteed price matrix with trusted clearing agents.

How We Operate

Farm to Foreign Customs

We partner directly with farmer clusters and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) across Gujarat and Maharashtra. We mandate specific pesticide guidelines to ensure our produce complies with European (EU) and GCC Maximum Residue Limits (MRLs).

"Our Exporting Style focuses on backward integration. By monitoring the farm and packhouse stages, we minimize delays and ensure product freshness upon arrival."

Payment Framework

  • Initial Phase: 100% Advance Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) required for the first 3 to 5 sample air-cargo batches.
  • Established Rhythm: Once a shipping history is established, we operate under:
    • Document Against Payment (DP) at Sight.
    • Irrevocable Letter of Credit (LC) confirmed by an international bank.
