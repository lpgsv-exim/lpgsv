How We Operate

Preservation Framework: From Farm to Foreign Customs

Our operational model bridges agricultural fields to sophisticated global border checkpoints through technical compliance and modern post-harvest engineering handling styles.

Sourcing & Farmer Alignment

Our structural exporting mechanism involves partnering directly with verified regional farmer clusters and established Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) across agricultural hubs in Gujarat and Maharashtra.

MRL Traceability Mandate

To secure frictionless customs clearance at arrival destinations, we provide farmers with exact crop guidelines detailing permitted pesticide classes. This ensures all final products strictly comply with European Union and GCC Maximum Residue Limits (MRLs).

Maritime Ocean Cargo

We route shipments seamlessly utilizing highly trusted clearing agents out of major Gujarat maritime freight gates. All ocean container movements are committed under a strict, predictable minimum guaranteed price matrix to insulate buyers against extreme freight spot fluctuations.

Priority Air Forwarding

For perishables needing expedited time handling, we maintain secure relationships with elite, IATA-approved freight forwarders at Ahmedabad and Mumbai hubs. This guarantees buyers a fixed, predictable space allotment on commercial departures every Tuesday and Thursday.

Commercial Payment Infrastructure

Structured trade finance milestones protecting supply integrity and relationship scalability.

Phase 1: Initial Trade Onboarding

To initiate workflow sync on the first 3 to 5 sample air-cargo batches, business transactions operate under 100% Advance Telegraphic Transfer (T/T) terms. This funds prompt regional allocations and dedicated premium packing runs.

Phase 2: Standard Trade Execution

Once a steady transactional rhythm is confirmed, we transition operations smoothly to flexible banking terms. Extended commercial lines include Document Against Payment (DP) at Sight or an Irrevocable Letter of Credit (LC) verified through major international banking networks.
